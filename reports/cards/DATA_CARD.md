# Data Card: GridSense

## Data purpose
Support local outage-risk forecasting and conditional disruption modeling for Addis Ababa.

## Included datasets
- `data/processed/community_outage_reports_clean.csv` (event-level cleaned community reports)
- `data/processed/gridsense_forecasting_dataset.csv` (window-level forecasting dataset)
- `data/collected/community_outage_reports.csv` (raw intake)
- `data/processed/local_evidence_dataset.csv` (public context indicators)

## Privacy
No names, phone numbers, emails, exact addresses, GPS coordinates, or free-text notes are included in the public processed datasets.

## Limitation
This is community-reported evidence, not full official grid telemetry. Coverage and participation can vary by time/location.
